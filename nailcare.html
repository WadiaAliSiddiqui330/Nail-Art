Nail Care Guide

Your complete guide to healthy, strong, and beautiful nails

Nail Care Basics

Essential steps for healthy and beautiful nails

Daily Cleaning

Wash your hands gently with mild soap and lukewarm water. Keep nails clean and dry to prevent bacteria growth.

Proper Trimming

Cut nails straight across and file in one direction. This prevents ingrown nails and reduces breakage.

Moisturize Daily

Apply cuticle oil and hand cream daily. Well-moisturized nails are stronger and more flexible.

Use Base Coat

Always apply a base coat before nail polish. It protects your nails and helps polish last longer.

Wear Gloves

Protect your nails when cleaning or gardening. Gloves prevent damage from harsh chemicals and dirt.

Healthy Diet

Eat foods rich in biotin, protein, and vitamins. Good nutrition supports strong nail growth from within.

Pro Tips for Perfect Nails

Expert advice for maintaining beautiful nails

1
File in One Direction

Always file from the outer edge toward the center. Back-and-forth filing can cause splits and weaken nails.

2
Don't Cut Cuticles

Push back cuticles gently instead of cutting them. Cutting can lead to infection and damage.

3
Take Polish Breaks

Give your nails a break from polish occasionally to let them breathe and strengthen naturally.

4
Use Quality Tools

Invest in good nail files, clippers, and buffers. Quality tools make a big difference in nail health.

5
Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water daily. Hydration affects nail health just like it affects your skin and hair.

6
Avoid Nail Biting

Keep nails trimmed short and apply bitter-tasting nail polish to break the habit of nail biting.

Common Nail Problems & Solutions

Click on each problem to see the solution

Brittle, Breaking Nails
Solutions:
  • Use a nail strengthener 2-3 times per week
  • Keep nails shorter until they strengthen
  • Wear gloves when doing household chores
  • Increase biotin in your diet or consider supplements
Ridged Nails
Solutions:
  • Gently buff ridges with a fine-grit buffer
  • Use ridge-filling base coats
  • Maintain consistent moisturizing routine
  • Consider vitamin supplements after consulting a doctor
Peeling Nails
Solutions:
  • Avoid acetone-based nail polish removers
  • Keep nails well-moisturized with cuticle oil
  • Limit exposure to water and harsh chemicals
  • Use a nail hardener to strengthen weak nails
Hangnails
Solutions:
  • Keep cuticles moisturized daily with cuticle oil
  • Carefully trim hangnails with clean cuticle nippers
  • Never pull or bite hangnails
  • Apply antibiotic ointment if area becomes infected
