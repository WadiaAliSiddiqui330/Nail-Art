    A Brief History of Nail Art

    Nail art has a rich history dating back over 5,000 years. In ancient Egypt, long, painted nails were a sign of royalty. Chinese aristocrats used nail colors to reflect social class as early as 3000 BC. Over centuries, the art evolved — from the French manicure in the 1970s to today’s limitless designs using gems, foils, and digital prints. What was once a cultural marker has become a global fashion statement. Nail art today blends tradition, innovation, and personal identity — and we're proud to be part of that journey.
